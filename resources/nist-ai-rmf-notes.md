# NIST AI Risk Management Framework — Notes

**Source:** https://airc.nist.gov/RMF_Map
**Date:** Day 1 — July 17, 2026

The framework has 4 functions:

**GOVERN** means: Establishes policies, roles and accountability, sets risk levels, values transparency, documentation, and improvement.

**MAP** means: Helps to understand context by understanding intended purpose, actors involved and affected, and identifying and listing potential risks.

**MEASURE** means: Gives us AI system insights by testing and evaluation in terms of accuracy, robustness, as well as sociotechnical factors like fairness, explainability, and privacy.

**MANAGE** means: It prioritizes findings, decides whether to proceed, modify, or retire a system/feature.
