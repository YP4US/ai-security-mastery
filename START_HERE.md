**Morning:** Open `daily-cards/day-001.md` → work through it → check boxes

## If You Fall Behind

**Don't skip days. Don't rename files to pretend you're ahead.**

If Day 1 takes 3 actual calendar days, that's fine. Just keep calling it Day 1 until the
Day 1 completion checklist is done. Then move to Day 2.

The goal is the learning, not the date.

---

## The One Metric That Matters

**GitHub commits.** Every day you complete, there's a commit. Open your GitHub repo in 30 days and count the commits. That number is your real progress.
