## HOW TO APPLY (Do this for every single role)

```
1. Find the hiring manager or a team member on LinkedIn → Connect with note
2. Research the company's AI initiatives (10–15 min Google + their website)  
3. Customize your resume summary (2 sentences max) for this role
4. Write a 3-paragraph cover letter (why them, why you, why now)
5. Apply through their portal
6. Follow up on LinkedIn message if no response in 7 days
```

## COVER LETTER TEMPLATE (customize for each role)

```
Subject: Application — [Role Title] | [Your Name]

Dear [Hiring Manager name — find on LinkedIn],

I'm applying for the [Role Title] position at [Company]. I've spent the last several 
years building and securing AI systems — publishing research on adversarial ML, 
building two AI-powered products under Canadian privacy law, and formally assessing 
my own systems against the NIST AI Risk Management Framework.

What makes me unusual for this role: I understand AI governance both from the 
framework side AND from the technical side. I know what prompt injection looks like, 
how training data poisoning works, and why RLHF is not the same as security. 
That combination — researcher, builder, and governance specialist — is rare, and 
it means I can translate between your technical teams, your risk function, and 
your regulators without losing either audience.

I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background applies to [Company]'s 
AI initiatives. I've attached my resume and a portfolio including a NIST AI RMF 
assessment of my own production AI system.

Yogesh Pawar
[LinkedIn] | [GitHub portfolio link] | [Email]
```

## TRACK A INTERVIEW QUESTIONS — PRACTICE THESE OUT LOUD

Practice answering each one in under 90 seconds. Time yourself.

1. "What is the NIST AI Risk Management Framework and how would you apply it?"
2. "How would you classify an AI hiring tool under the EU AI Act?"
3. "What's the difference between AI safety and AI security?"
4. "Walk me through how you'd build an AI governance program from scratch."
5. "How would you assess the AI risk of a third-party vendor we're considering?"
6. "What is a model card and why does it matter?"
7. "How does PIPEDA apply to AI systems that process personal health data?"
8. "What is indirect prompt injection and why is it a governance concern?"
9. "How would you explain AI hallucination risk to a CFO?"
10. "You discover our AI system is producing biased outputs. Walk me through your response."
