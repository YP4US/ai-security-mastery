## 🎯 THE VIZUARA SERIES DECISION (Read This)

**Playlist:** "Building LLMs from Scratch" by Vizuara (43 videos)
**Content:** Transformer architecture, tokenization, attention math, coding GPT-2

**Verdict: YES — include it. Here is exactly why and when:**

It fits into **Ch04 (Days 44–57)** as the technical backbone, NOT as a prerequisite for Track A. Here is the logic:

- For **Track A (Governance):** You do NOT need to build an LLM to govern AI risk. You need to understand how LLMs *behave* and *fail* — not implement them from scratch. This series gives you depth that makes you credible when a CISO asks "so how does attention actually work?" — that's the difference between a policy person and a technical governance expert.

- For **Track B (Security):** Understanding transformer internals is directly relevant to adversarial attacks. Knowing *how* tokenization works explains *why* certain prompt injections succeed. This is not optional background — it is the mathematical foundation of everything in Ch05.

- **Scheduling fit:** 43 videos × avg ~25 min = ~18 hours total. Watching 2 videos per day at 11:15–12:00 covers it in 22 days. It fits perfectly into the morning-block gap between Ch01/Ch02 labs and the Track A afternoon block — Weeks 1–4.

**Do NOT watch it all before starting. Watch 2 per day as scheduled.**
