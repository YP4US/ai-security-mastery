## 💰 CERTIFICATION DECISION (Updated)

| Cert | Cost | Decision | Timing |
|------|------|----------|--------|
| AIGP | $799 USD (~$1,100 CAD) | **DEFER** — portfolio replaces it | After first paycheck |
| CompTIA Security+ | ~$380 CAD | **DO IT** — high ROI, opens Track B | Aug 15 if budget allows, else Sep 1 |
| AWS SAA-C03 | ~$165 CAD | **DO IT** — low cost, high signal | Sep 15 |
| HTB AI Red Teamer | Free | **DO IT** | Oct 1 |

**Why AIGP is deferred, not skipped:**
Track A employers care about *demonstrated knowledge* of NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, and AI risk frameworks. Your portfolio (Mediqrate risk assessment, EU AI Act technical file, Canadian regulation cheatsheet) proves this directly. The AIGP proves you *studied* it. Portfolio > certification for someone with your research background. Get the cert once you have income — it will still help for salary negotiation in your second role.

**Why Security+ is still recommended despite tight budget:**
At ~$380 CAD, Security+ has the best ROI of any cert in your plan. It opens Canadian government roles (CSE, DND, Treasury Board), signals to bank security teams, and is often a literal checkbox on job applications. If budget is genuinely tight, delay it to September and apply for Track A first — but prioritize getting it before Track B applications begin.
